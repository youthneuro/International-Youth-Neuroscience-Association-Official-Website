Brain Bee Bootcamp 2025

Organized by IYNA in partnership with the International Brain Bee (IBB), the Brain Bee Bootcamp offers focused prep for participants at all levels—local, regional, or national—from around the world.

Learn More ›
Lecturers Section

Submission Details

The bootcamp follows the IBB World Championship format with sections on:
  • Written exam
  • Patient diagnosis
  • Neuroanatomy
  • Neurohistology
You’ll also hear from past IBB finalists and neuroscience experts, who’ll share study strategies and insights to help you succeed.
Dates: 4 weekends, from January 11 to February 2, 2025
Before registering: Please review the full Brain Bee Bootcamp Info Packet for details.

Professor Charles Watson

FOCUS: BRAIN MAPPING, CORTEX MAPPING

Professor Charles Watson is an eminent neurobiologist and public health physician with over 35 years’ experience teaching anatomy to medical and science students. During his tenure as chief health officer of Western Australia from 1984 to 1994, he established campaigns for quit, HIV/AIDS, and breast cancer screening and was appointed their Chief Health Officer in 1993. Charles holds an emeritus professorial position at Curtin University and has authored or co-authored 25 books on neuroscience, including his renowned atlas of the rat brain, cited over 10,000 times. He has published over 100 journal articles and was made a Member of the Order of Australia in 2004 for his contributions to brain research and public health.
