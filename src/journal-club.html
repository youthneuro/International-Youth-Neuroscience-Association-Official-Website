IYNA Research Journal Club

The IYNA Research Journal Club is a club where members critically evaluate and discuss scientific articles and publications, with a focus on improving understanding of neuroscience-related studies. We aim to keep members informed about the latest research, encourage critical thinking, foster collaboration, and enhance skills in reading and presenting scientific literature. Our Research Journal Club will meet once a month where we will be discussing a Neuroscience-related topic within the scope of a Current Researcher's field or research paper. By joining our research club you will have the opportunity to present a research paper and have a researcher answers questions with regards to their research afterwards to a large audience. Our once-per-month virtual meetings offer you an opportunity to read and discuss scientific literature and newsworthy developments in the field of neuroscience in a low-pressure environment, with expert scientists from the field of Neuroscience. There are no coursework prerequisites for participation, just a curiosity for science and a desire to learn how to understand scientific literature! After filling out this form, you will receive a follow-up email, within a couple of weeks, with more detailed information regarding the journal club and the specific dates and times of our meetings.
