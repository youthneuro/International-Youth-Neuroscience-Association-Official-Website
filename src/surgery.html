Neurosurgery Series

Neurosurgery Walk-Through Webinar

Are you interested in seeing brain surgery firsthand? Do you want to understand the intricate thought processes of surgeons in high-stakes situations?

Join us on Saturday, August 26th, 2023 at 11 AM GMT and 11 PM GMT for an immersive and educational event led by professional neurosurgeons; the Neurosurgery Walk-Through Webinar! Participants will have the opportunity to dive head-first into the field of neuroscience while witnessing a live, walk-through video of a surgery, supplemented by insightful commentary and thorough analysis of surgical techniques and procedures. The video will be followed by a Q&A session, where participants will have their neurosurgery questions addressed by an expert panel. This is an invaluable experience for students wishing to enter the fascinating field of neurosurgery.
