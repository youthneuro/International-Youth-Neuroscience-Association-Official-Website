Chapter System

Chapters

An IYNA Chapter is a self-governing group of students generally based at schools or local communities. Any community that actively engages with neuroscience is eligible to become a Chapter! Chapters may receive support in the form of grants from IYNA, but they are ultimately responsible for organizing their own meetings and activities.
