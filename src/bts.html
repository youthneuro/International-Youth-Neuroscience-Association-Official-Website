Events Team

Aditi Dewangan

Department: Journal

Events Co-Director

Aditi is a high school junior in Seattle, Washington. Through her work in IYNA, she hopes to inspire students in the field of neuroscience by making events and competitions more efficient and accessible. Her role includes liaising with IYNA partner organizations. Additionally, she reaches out to high schools and universities to share the IYNA organization so that more students can access IYNA's resources. In her leisure time, she enjoys biking and volunteering at her local senior home.

Steven Jin

Department: Events

Events Co-Director

Steven is a high school student from Texas. He hopes to promote more accessible neuroscience resources to all people from socioeconomic disadvantaged backgrounds through competitions, summer programs, webinars, and bootcamps. His role includes creating new events, organizing existing virtual and in-person events, as well as managing volunteers. In his free time, he enjoys playing tennis and listening to all genres of music.


Youth Neuroscience Summer Program

Eve Harrison

Department: Events

Head TA

Eve is a post-graduate research student in Scotland, UK. Having just finished her Master's Degree in Brain Sciences at the University of Glasgow, she is now pursuing her PhD in Cognitive Neuroscience & Psychology at the University of the West of Scotland. Her research focuses on changes in perceptual decision making during aging and Alzheimers Disease. Through her work with IYNA, she hopes to help as many budding-neuroscientists as possible to enter the field and find the same passion that she has through her own studies. Her specific roles include developing the IYNA’s Youth Neuroscience Summer Program, judging essay writing competitions, and being the Head Teaching Assistant in the Youth Neuroscience Summer Course. In her spare time, Eve enjoys reading, diamond painting, watching movies and playing with her pet rats and dog.
