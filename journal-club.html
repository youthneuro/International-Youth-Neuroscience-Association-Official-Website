What Is the Journal Club?

A virtual club where students gather once a month to:

  • Read neuroscience-related research papers
  • Discuss findings and ask questions
  • Hear from guest scientists and current researchers
  • Learn how to critically evaluate and present scientific literature

Who Can Join?

  • Open to high school and undergraduate students
  • No coursework or prior research experience required
  • Ideal for anyone curious about neuroscience and science writing

Why Participate?

Perfect for students exploring careers in neuroscience, surgery, or medicine.
