What is the International Youth Neuroscience Association?

The IYNA serves over 4,000 members and has reached students in over 100 countries. Through its fully-online Introduction to Neuroscience summer course, themed speaker events, and global chapter system, the IYNA aims to make the field of neuroscience accessible to all.
