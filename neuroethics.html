Our Partners

Collaborating with leading organizations to advance neuroscience education and research worldwide

International Neuroethics Society

(INS)

The INS is an interdisciplinary group of scholars, scientists, clinicians, and professionals dedicated to inspiring research and discussion on the responsible use of advances in brain science.

International Youth Neuroscience Association

(IYNA)

The IYNA is a global, youth-led nonprofit promoting neuroscience education. The IYNA Journal publishes articles on neuroethics and neuroscience and supports a worldwide network of over 1,000 members in 126 countries.
